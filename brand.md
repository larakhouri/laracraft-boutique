# Lara Craft Gifts - Brand Pillars

## 1. Bespoke Portal
Custom-commissioned items where customers can request personalized gifts tailored to specific needs or occasions. Focus on uniqueness and craftsmanship.

## 2. Artisan Boutique
A curated collection of ready-made handcrafted goods. These items are distinct, high-quality, and reflect the artisan's personal touch and style.

## 3. POD Merch (Print-on-Demand)
Scalable merchandise featuring original designs applied to various products (t-shirts, mugs, totes). Allows for broader reach with lower inventory risk.

## 4. Photo Gallery
A visual showcase of past works, work-in-process shots, and the creative journey. Serves as a portfolio and inspiration board for potential bespoke clients.

## 5. Maker Supplies
Sales of supplies, kits, or tools for other makers. Building a community around the craft by sharing resources and materials.
